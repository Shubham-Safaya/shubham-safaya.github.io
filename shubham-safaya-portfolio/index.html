About

From Jammu to Walmart Global Tech — via KPMG, Duke, and a YouTube Channel

I'm a Product Manager who believes the best products emerge when you deeply understand both the data and the human behind it. My journey started in consulting at KPMG India, where I learned to think in frameworks and stakeholder maps. Duke's MEM program (GPA 3.9, PM track, Ex-President) gave me the engineering-meets-business lens. JM Family Enterprises taught me analytics product craft. And Walmart — Walmart taught me scale.

Today I build third-party data products that power retail media advertising — negotiating vendor partnerships, designing identity resolution systems, and ensuring privacy compliance at enterprise scale. Outside work, I host Sundays with Safaya on YouTube and Instagram, helping people navigate careers in tech.
